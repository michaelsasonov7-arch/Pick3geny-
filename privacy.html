Pick3Geny — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

Pick3Geny ("the App") is a free mathematical number pattern analysis tool intended for entertainment purposes only. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Pick3Geny does not collect, transmit, store on servers, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Device contacts or media
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you enter into Pick3Geny — including your number inputs, run history, statistics, and settings — is stored exclusively on your own device using browser localStorage technology. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by using the "Clear All" button inside the app, or by clearing your browser/app data from your device settings.

4. Advertising

Pick3Geny displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use certain device information (such as advertising ID) to serve relevant advertisements. This data collection is governed entirely by Google's own Privacy Policy.

For more information about how Google uses data, visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

This App is not directed at children under the age of 18. If you are under 18 years of age, you must not use this App. By using Pick3Geny, you confirm that you meet the applicable age requirement in your jurisdiction.
